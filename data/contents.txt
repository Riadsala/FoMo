Datasets from the following papers:

Bhat 2025: As yet unpublished dataset where participants forage on a rotated grid.

Clarke 2022: Stable individual differences in strategies, within, but not between, visual search tasks.
Full dataset available at https://osf.io/y6qbv/.

Kristjansson 2014: Common attentional constraints in visual foraging.
Full information available at https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0100752.

Tagu 2022: The selection balance: contrasting value, proximity and priming in a multitarget foraging task.
Full dataset available at https://osf.io/e7cnd/https://osf.io/e7cnd/.

Hughes 2024: The effect of target scarcity on visual foraging.
Full information available at https://github.com/Riadsala/foraging_scarcity.

Wolfe 2012: personal communication data from Jeremy Wolfe, foraging data from circa 2012.
